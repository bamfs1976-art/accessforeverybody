About Access for Every Body

Access for Every Body is a community-driven venue guide helping plus-size people, wheelchair users, and anyone with access needs discover which UK venues truly welcome them.

We believe nobody should have to arrive at a venue only to discover it doesn't accommodate their body. Our ratings cover:

  • Seating comfort — seat widths, armrest types, alternative seating
  • Physical access — step-free entry, aisle widths, lift availability
  • Accessible toilets — Changing Places, space, grab rails
  • Staff helpfulness — attitude, training, willingness to assist

Every rating comes from real community members who have visited the venue. We are not affiliated with any venue listed. Always confirm your specific needs directly with the venue before visiting.

Accessibility statement: We are committed to making this website itself accessible. If you encounter any barriers using this site, please contact us so we can improve.
